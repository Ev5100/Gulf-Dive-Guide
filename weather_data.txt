<p><span class="fcst-day">SYNOPSIS</span><br>

afternoon and evening north of the Yucatan Peninsula and in the
eastern Bay of Campeche as a diurnal trough develops and treks
westward across the region. Otherwise, ridging over the western
Atlantic will maintain moderate S to SE winds and moderate seas
over the central and western Gulf of America through the middle of
next week, with gentle to locally moderate SE winds and slight to
moderate seas in the eastern basin.</p>
